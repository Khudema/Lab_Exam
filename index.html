Air University


INTRODUCTION

The Air University (AU) is a federally chartered public sector research university in Pakistan.Established in 2002, its main campus is located in the capital city of Islamabad, Pakistan.
The university has two other functional campuses, Aerospace and Aviation Campus at Kamra and a campus at Multan.The university offers undergraduate and post-graduate degrees in
artificial intelligence, business management, computer science, cyber security, engineering, medicine, and humanities.
