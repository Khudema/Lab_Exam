Air university


INTRODUCTION

The Air University (AU) is a federally chartered public sector research university in Pakistan.Established in
2002, its main campus is located in the capital city of Islamabad, Pakistan. The university has two other
functional campuses, Aerospace and Aviation Campus at Kamra and a campus at Multan.The university
offers undergraduate and post-graduate degrees in artificial intelligence, business management, computer
science, cyber security, engineering, medicine, and humanities.

Campuses

Islamabad Campus

The campus is located near the Margalla foothills in the south east corner of sector E-9 (PAF Complex) of Islamabad established in 2002.

Kamra Campus

The Air University or AU is a public research university located in Kamra, Pakistan and established in 2017.

Multan Campus

Air University Multan was established in 2011 with a noble purpose of serving the region in the field of education.

Kharian Campus
